Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first event?
Open the Rondevu Management app, tap the "+" button, fill in your event details including title, date, time, location, and description. Add ticket types and pricing, then publish your event to make it live.
How do I scan tickets at my event?
Navigate to your event in the app, tap "Scan Tickets", and use your device's camera to scan attendee QR codes. The app will verify ticket validity and mark them as checked in.
How do I set up ticket pricing and types?
When creating or editing an event, go to the "Tickets" section. You can create multiple ticket types (General Admission, VIP, Early Bird, etc.) with different prices and availability limits.
How do I manage my venue profile?
Go to your profile tab, select "Venue Settings", and update your venue information including capacity, amenities, photos, and operating hours. This helps event organizers find and book your venue.
How do I track ticket sales and revenue?
Access the "Analytics" section for each event to view real-time ticket sales, revenue, attendee demographics, and sales trends. You can export reports for accounting purposes.
How do payouts work?
Ticket revenue is processed through Stripe and automatically transferred to your connected bank account. Payouts typically occur 2-7 business days after the event, minus processing fees.
Can I offer refunds to customers?
Yes, you can process refunds directly through the app. Go to your event, select "Manage Tickets", find the order, and tap "Refund". Refunds are processed through Stripe.
How do I promote my events?
Your events are automatically listed in the Rondevu consumer app. You can also share event links on social media, enable early bird pricing, and use the built-in promotional tools.

Technical Support

Experiencing technical issues? Try these steps:

  • Update to the latest version of Rondevu Management
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Ensure camera permissions are enabled for QR scanning
  • Clear app cache (iOS: offload app in Settings)
  • If issues persist, contact us at jgui@rondevuevents.com
