Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, you will lose access to all your events, venues, organizations, and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to request account deletion:

  1. Open the Rondevu Management app on your device
  2. Go to Settings/Profile (usually accessible from the main menu)
  3. Look for "Account Settings" or "Privacy Settings"
  4. Select "Delete Account" option
  5. Confirm your identity (you may need to re-enter your password)
  6. Review the deletion consequences and confirm deletion

Alternative Method

If you cannot access the app or need assistance, you can request account deletion by contacting our support team using the contact information below.

  • Privacy Settings: Adjust privacy settings instead of full deletion

After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to access the Rondevu Management app with your credentials
  • All events, venues, and organizations you created will be permanently removed
  • Other users will no longer be able to find or interact with your content
  • Any pending transactions or bookings will be cancelled
  • You will need to create a new account if you want to use our services again
